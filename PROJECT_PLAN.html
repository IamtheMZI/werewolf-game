üìã Project Overview

A web-based multiplayer Werewolf card game inspired by Jackbox Party Pack mechanics. Players join sessions via room codes, receive role assignments, and interact through text prompts and tap-to-reveal mechanics instead of traditional audio narration.

Create or join game rooms with unique codes. Host controls game start and configuration.

üë• Dynamic Role Assignment

üé≠ Game Roles & Mechanics

üê∫ Werewolf

Werewolf Team

Goal: Hide your identity and avoid being voted out.

Night Action: Wake with other werewolves to see teammates. No card view/swap.

Win Condition: At least one werewolf survives if one is killed in voting.

üåô Dream Wolf

Werewolf Team

Goal: Same as werewolf but doesn't know other werewolves.

Night Action: Sleeps alone, doesn't wake with werewolves.

üòà Minion

Werewolf Team

Goal: Help werewolves win without being a werewolf.

Night Action: Sees who the werewolves are, but they don't see the minion.

Win Condition: Wins if werewolf team wins, even if killed.

üîÆ Seer

Village Team

Goal: Use information to help village find werewolves.

Night Action: Look at one other player's card OR two center cards.

üîÑ Robber

Village Team (initially)

Goal: May change based on stolen role.

Night Action: Swap your card with another player's and view your new card.

üò¥ Drunk

Village Team (initially)

Goal: Uncertain - role changes but unknown.

Night Action: Must swap card with center without looking at new role.

üßù Insomniac

Village Team

Goal: Help village, knowing if role was changed.

Night Action: Wakes at end of night to check if their card was swapped.

üòá Villager

Village Team

Goal: Help identify and vote out werewolves.

Night Action: None - sleeps through the night.

üÉè Tanner

Neutral

Goal: Get yourself killed in the voting.

Night Action: None.

Win Condition: Only wins if voted out. Everyone else loses.

üéÆ Game Flow

1. Pre-Game Phase

  • Host creates game room with unique code
  • Players join using room code
  • Lobby displays connected players
  • Host selects role configuration
  • Host starts game when ready

2. Setup Phase

  • System assigns roles randomly
  • Each player views their card privately
  • 3 cards placed in center (not revealed)
  • Game proceeds to night phase

3. Night Phase (Sequential Role Actions)

  • Werewolves: Wake to see each other (tap to reveal teammates)
  • Minion: Wakes to see werewolves (tap to reveal)
  • Seer: Views 1 player card OR 2 center cards (tap to reveal)
  • Robber: Swaps card with player and views new role (tap player ‚Üí swap ‚Üí reveal)
  • Drunk: Swaps card with center without looking (tap center ‚Üí swap)
  • Insomniac: Views own card at end of night (tap to check)

4. Day Phase

  • All players wake up
  • Discussion period (configurable timer)
  • Players discuss and accuse
  • Voting begins when timer ends or host triggers
  • Each player votes for one person
  • Player(s) with most votes are eliminated

5. Resolution & Win Conditions

  • All final roles revealed
  • Village Wins: If at least one werewolf is killed
  • Werewolf Wins: If no werewolves are killed
  • Tanner Wins: If Tanner is killed (everyone else loses)
  • Special: If no werewolves in play, village must vote no one
